Forum Rules:

  1. Respect Others:
    • Be courteous and respectful to fellow members.
    • Avoid personal attacks, insults, or offensive language.
  2. No Hate Speech or Harassment:
    • Do not post content that promotes discrimination, hate speech, or harassment.
    • Treat all members with dignity and respect, regardless of their background.
  3. Stay on Topic:
    • Keep discussions relevant to the forum's purpose.
    • Start new threads for new topics and contribute to existing ones.
  4. No Spamming:
    • Avoid excessive self-promotion or spamming of links.
    • Do not post the same content repeatedly.
  5. No Illegal Content:
    • Do not post or request any illegal or unethical content.
    • Respect copyright laws and give credit where it's due.
  6. Use Appropriate Language:
    • Keep language and tone appropriate for all audiences.
    • Refrain from using excessive profanity.
  7. Respect Privacy:
    • Do not share personal information about yourself or others.
    • Respect the privacy of others, both on and off the forum.
  8. Report Inappropriate Content:
    • Report any content that violates the forum rules to the moderators.
    • Help maintain a positive community by reporting inappropriate behavior.
  9. Moderator Instructions:
    • Follow the instructions of forum moderators.
    • Contact moderators privately for specific concerns or disputes.
  10. Multiple Accounts:
    • Users should only have one account. Multiple accounts can lead to a ban.
  11. Be Constructive:
    • Do not post inflammatory or off-topic messages with the intent to provoke others.
    • Provide constructive feedback and contribute positively to discussions.
    • Avoid "trolling" or posting content without substance.
  12. Understand and Follow UC Student Rules:
    • Adhere to any additional rules or guidelines within accordance to the student handbook and code of conduct.
